Roadmap

Q3-Q4 2017

- Release whitepaper
- Design Beenest & Bee Protocols
- Launch alpha prototype on testnet
- Partner with smart contract auditing firms
- Finalize partnerships with listing providers in SF

- Launch MVP in SF focusing on crypto hacker houses
- Integrate Beenest with 3rd party KYC platform
- Support payment and payout using BEE token

Q3-Q4 2018

- Further expand in SF with more A/B testing
- Launch Bee Protocols on mainnet
- Integrate Bee Protocols with 2-5 dApps
- Launch iOS and Android Beenest apps
- Integrate with decentralized KYC
- Integrate with decentralized exchanges so Beenest can accept ETH/BTC and fiat
- Integrate with a marketplace protocol to decentralize all listings data

- Implement governance model for future protocol improvements

2020+

- Further expansion in key cities worldwide
- Further integration with sharing economy dApps
