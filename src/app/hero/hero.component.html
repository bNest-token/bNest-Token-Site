About Bee

We are a team of engineers from Google, Facebook, and Uber reimagining short-term housing rentals. Current platforms charge exorbitant transaction fees and provide less than ideal user experiences.

Beenest is a blockchain-based open-source decentralized housing platform that utilizes the Bee Token. Beenest eliminates the middleman, allowing 0% commissions while providing a seamless user experience. Our vision is to revitalize the travel industry and put control back in the hands of the users.

What is Beenest

Beenest is a short-term housing rental platform for Hosts to list their homes and for Guests to find accommodations.

Why Beenest

Using the security of the Blockchain and the power of Smart Contracts, Beenest eliminates the middleman through decentralization, resulting in 0% commissions and a seamless user experience.

What is the Bee Token

The Bee Token is the cryptocurrency that fuels Beenest and provides an ever growing value to users.
