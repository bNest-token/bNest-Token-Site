The Bee Token: Reinventing Housing Exchange

Putting housing back into the hands of the consumer through the power of the Ethereum blockchain.

About Bee

Finding housing has become a nightmare riddled with scams, fees, and hidden costs. With the Bee Token, we are creating a safe housing exchange platform powered by Ethereum and our own side chain to make affordable, decentralized short-term housing a reality.

Created by engineers from Google and Uber, Bee and by extension the Bee Token strive to put the power back into the hands of the consumers by cutting out the middle man and pushing the savings and benefits back to the homeowners and renters.

What is Beenest

Beenest is a short-term home sharing service where Hosts can list their homes for Guests who are looking to book.

How Beenest works

Using security of the Blockchain to provide transparency and the power of Smart Contracts to make sure Hosts are paid, Beenest lets Hosts and Guests share homes safely.

What is the Bee Token

The Bee Token is the cryptocurrency that Beenest will operate on. With the ability to increase in value over time, it provides an ever growing value to Hosts and Guests who use Beenest.
